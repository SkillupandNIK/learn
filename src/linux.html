    traceroute
    Linux traceroute is one of the most useful commands in networking. It is used to troubleshoot the network. It detects the delay and determines the pathway to your target. It basically helps in the following ways: It provides the names and identifies every device on the path. It follows the route to the destination It determines where the network latency comes from and reports it.
